Our Story

Where three cuisines
share one fire.

Caspian Flame was born from a simple belief: the best food in the world sits where cultures meet. From the olive groves of the Mediterranean, across the bread-and-spice markets of Anatolia, to the herb-laden kitchens of the Caucasus — every dish on our menu carries a passport.

We hand-stretch our pide. We slow-marinate AAA beef. We chop tantuni to order on a hot plate. And we wrap it all in lavash baked the same morning.
